My Passions & Focus.

My passion lies in storytelling, in creating all types of media and web content that incite innovation, further a message and enable change. In the humanitarian field, the right storytelling helps humanitarian workers to realise their mandate and aid those that need most. In other fields, storytelling enables people to connect with a product, an idea or a solution that make their lives better, their families happier and their community thrive.

I like to think of me as the conductor who builds and orchestrates the team of skilled artists who can give life to media content with the purpose of improving lives and aiding solution-building efforts across the globe.

My focus is to look for talented artists and creatives from all walks of life to build effective and high performing teams.
